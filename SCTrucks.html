SC Trucks

This project was created for ACM's TrojanHacks 2017. See the devpost here!

Check out our presentation as well!

SCTrucks is an Android-based mobile application that allow users to locate food trucks in the nearby area. The app provides directions to the desired food truck as well as ratings, hours of opperation, and relavent tweets regarding the food truck of choice. We made use of both Yelp's and Twitter's public APIs to provide the data associated with each food truck and the GoogleMaps public API to direct our users reliably to their food truck of choice.

I worked on backend development, as I worked with the Yelp Business and Search APIs. I used this to create "FoodTruck" objects that were passed on to the rest of the application. I also worked on the Android implementation.
