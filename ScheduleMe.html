ScheduleMe

Check out our presentation!

ScheduleMe is a browser-based application aiming to automatically generate all possible schedules based on the userâ€™s course load and personal preferences. We wanted to make scheduling classes as easy as possible while also adding a social aspect via Facebook friends. We used a variety of languages and tools in the development of our application, such as Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap), Python, MySQL, and Apache Tomcat. Our application relies on multi-threading and networking to add functionality. Our algorithm is to generate valid permutations that fit with an user's constraints and cross-reference the most recent schedule already generated to create the new schedule. Users can login to Facebook and store their schedule, allowing their friends to then register and see classes in common.

I worked on backend development, helping with the algorithm and servlets. I also worked on the database structure and ensured we maintained the information we wanted.
